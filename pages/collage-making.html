COLLAGE MAKING

Fees


  • Collage Making : ₹ 50 per Person

Eligibility Criteria


The contest is open for B.E/B.Tech and Polytechnic level students.

Introduction


Collage is a group competition where a piece of art made by sticking various different materials such as photographs and pieces of paper or fabric on to a backing.

Purpose


To enhance participants ability in art making as well as to present it, and also to broaden their mind.

Rules & Regulations


  • Material will not be provided by the institute. Except drawing sheet as a base.

  • Judging would be on the basis of creative collage making with the help of photographs and paper cutting.

  • Only paper cutting collage will not be considered.

  • Decision of the judges and the event heads shall be treated as final.

  • All the candidates will get participation certificates.

  • The participants should come with their college ID Card.

  • Spot Entries are also accepted.

  • For any clarification or further details, the interested candidates can contact the Faculty or Student coordinators.

  • All the rights are reserved with IGNITE 2024 event committee.

Team Formation


  • Team can have a maximum of 3 participants from B.Tech./B.E./Diploma.

  • No participants can be part of more than one team.

  • It is not necessary that the participants forming a team should be from the same college.

  • There is no restriction on the number of teams from the same college.
