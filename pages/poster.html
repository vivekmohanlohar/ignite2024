POSTER PRESENTATON

Eligibility Criteria


The contest is open for B.E/B.Tech and Polytechnic level students.

Introduction


Step into the world of ideas and innovation with our poster presentation. Engage visually as we showcase research, insights, and creativity in a concise and impactful display.

Purpose


Innovative ideas that leads to practical purpose.

Rules & Regulations


  • Maximum two students are allowed.

  • Poster size: 56 x 38cm (drawing sheet size).

  • Poster should be innovative and attractive based on engineering themes.

  • Any types of colors can be used.

  • Printed banners are not allowed.

  • On-spot registration is allowed.

  • No participants can be part of more than one team of the same event.

  • There are no restrictions on the number of teams from the same college.

  • Participants forming a team may be from the same or different colleges.

  • Jury decision will be the final decision.

  • All the rights are reserved with IGNITE 2023 event committee.
