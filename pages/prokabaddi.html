Introduction


Pro Kabaddi League (also known as Vivo Pro Kabbadi for sponsorship reasons) or abbreviated to PKL is an Indian men's professional Kabaddi league. It was launched in 2014 and is broadcast on Star Sports. It is the most popular kabaddi league in the world.

Rules & Regulations


  • Each team consists of 7 players. One substitute is allowed. Total players are only 8.

  • Super tackle is allowed. (1+1=2)

  • Up to 23 years old student is eligible to participate in this event.

  • Super Raid is allowed. (3+1)

  • 3rd Raid is Do or Die.

  • Weight Limit is 80 Kg.

  • Umpire decision will be final. Any objection on the decision will not be entertained.

  • If any misbehavior is observed during the match, then the team will be disqualified.

  • The time duration for each match will be 2 halves of 7 minutes.

  • Raider must start his cant before he touches the opponent’s court.

  • For every session, each team will be allowed to take one timeout of 30 sec.

  • Nails of the players must be closely clipped & no ornaments of any sort will be allowed to wear during playtime.

  • Oil or greasy material must not be applied while playing.

  • Participants of polytechnic or engineering are only allowed. I-Card of the current year is mandatory. Without I-Card, participants are not allowed.

  • Undertaking will be taken from each team before starting the match.

  • All rights are reserved with the college and IGNITE 2023.

  • All standard rules regarding Kabaddi are applicable.

  • Match slots will be decided on 17th Jan. 2023 by a lucky draw system.
