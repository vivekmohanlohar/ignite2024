TALENT HUNT

Price


  • 1st Price : ₹ 1500

  • 2nd Price : ₹ 1000

Fees


  • Fees : ₹ 100 per Group (2 participants per group)

Eligibility Criteria


  • The contest is open for B.E/B. Tech and Polytechnic level students.

    • Purpose


  • Let the rolling of brain race with every passing of minute.

  • The purpose of the quiz contest is to motivate the skills, abilities, and knowledge of students.

    • General Guideline


    1. Mobile phone strictly prohibited

    2. Use of internet is prohibited

    Rules & Regulations


    • Single or group entries are allowed. A group shall consist of a maximum of two participants.

    • The participants shall not be allowed to use mobile or other electronic instruments.

    • Decisions of the judges are final.

    • Substitutions are forbidden during the contest.

    • If an answer be interjected from the audience, the question may be discarded at the official's discretion.

    Judging Criteria


  • 1st round: Aptitude Test (Optional, conducted if entries exceed the required number)

  • Total of 20 multiple-choice questions with one mark each
  • Questions based on general knowledge and logical reasoning
  • Time limit: 15 minutes
  • No negative marking
  • One group member must take the test; both members proceed to the second round if qualified
  • 2nd round: Rapid Fire Round (For teams qualifying from the 1st round)

  • 5 questions based on general knowledge asked to each team (10 marks per question)
  • Time limit: 2 minutes to answer all questions
  • Unanswered questions are skipped (no penalty)
  • Only teams finishing the round within the time limit progress to the next round
  • No multiple-choice options provided for questions
  • No negative marking in this round
  • 3rd Round: Buzzer Round (For teams qualifying from the 2nd round)

  • Questions are multiple-choice with 10 marks each
  • Teams press buzzers to answer first
  • Negative marking: -5 marks for incorrect answers
  • 4 th Round: Surprise Round
  • The rules and details shall be provided at the time of round only.
