CAD WAR

Conditions


Eligibility Criteria: The competition is open for B.E. /B. Tech and Polytechnic level students only.

Time: Total 60 min. will be given.

Team Size: Maximum 6 members are allowed in a Team.

Introduction


If you have ability to draw anything on the 2D Software then this event is for you. This event is testing your drafting and modelling skills. An object is given at the time of event and student has to draw the object in stipulated time.

Rules & Regulations


  • There will be two rounds; one will be of 30 min, and round two will be of 60 min each.

  • The drawings of each event will be provided at the event.

  • The student has to draw the drawing in AutoCAD software.

  • The marks will be allotted to each drawing based on:
    • Completeness of drawing
    • Time taken to complete the drawing
    • Dimensions given to drawing
    • Nomenclature

  • All participants are required to report 30 min before the event starts, to the reporting desk.

  • Every participant should have his college identity card and registration receipt.

  • Any misbehavior of a participant during the event or round may lead to elimination from the round.

  • No extra time will be given to anyone under any circumstance.

  • Judges' decision will be final.

  • Other instructions will be provided on the day of the event.

  • All rights are reserved with ignite committee.

  • The drawing having incompleteness, right proportion, and high score point will be awarded the prize.

  • Assume any missing dimensions in the drawing.

Scoring Pattern is as follows


  • Design: 10 points

  • Dimensioning: 10 points

  • Line type: 10 points

  • Symbols: 10 points

  • Timing: 10 points
