Fees

Eligibility Criteria


  • The one who can crack Aptitude, Group Discussion and Personal Interview

    • Purpose


    MOCK Placement is an exciting opportunity to students for aptitude test, communication skills for group discussion and knowledge for personal interview. Put your skills to the test and advance with new excitement in next level.

    Rules & Regulations

    Round 1 (Online Aptitude Test)


    • In this round, participants will have to take an online Aptitude test.

    • Time allowed for the test is 30 minutes.

    • Participants who reach the passing criteria (40%) will proceed to the next round.

    Round 2 (Group Discussion)

    • This round consists of a Group discussion on Current affairs.

    • The time allotted is 10 minutes for each group of 10 students.(First 2 minutes: Understanding of the topic, 7 minutes for discussion among all participants, and the last 1 minute for conclusion).

    • Participants who reach the minimum marks as per the criteria will proceed to the next round.

    Parameters
    • Politeness

    • Confidence

    • English speaking

    • Cooperativeness

    • Body Language

    • Proper eye contact

    Round 3 (Personal Interview)

    • In this round, participants will have to give a Personal Interview.

    • Winners and runners-up will be selected based on the Personal Interview (PI) scores obtained by participants.

    • The interview will be conducted by experts from the college or outside.

    Parameters
    • Politeness & attitude

    • Confidence

    • English speaking

    • Proper eye contact

    • Body Language

    • Resume

    Genral Guide


    • Each student can register only once.

    • In case of a tie, 'Fastest finger first' game will be played.

    • The competition will be executed in 3 rounds.

    • Cell phones must be switched off.

    • Participants are not allowed to bring any media devices such as Cell Phones, Smart Phones, etc.

    • Apart from these rules, additional rules will be disclosed on the spot.

    • Any violation of rules will lead to disqualification.

    • All decisions made by the event heads will be final.
