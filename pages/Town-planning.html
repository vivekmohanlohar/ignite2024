TOWN PLANNING

Fees


  • Registration Fees : ₹ 50 per participant

Eligibility Criteria


The contest is open for B.E/B.Tech and Polytechnic level students.

Problem Statement


Area of land is 5 acres, in this area need to prepare a plan of town. In Planning mainly Residence sector, public sector ( Hospitals,Schools,,Colleges,Banks,Groceries,Temple,Gardens,Police Station Fire Station) ,Industrial Sector must be included.

Condition


  • Eligibility Criteria: The competition is open for B.E. /B. Tech and Polytechnic level students only.

  • Material to be used: Drawing Sheet will be provided by organizers. (Bring your own scale, pencil, eraser, and pen with you.)

  • Team Size: Maximum 2 members are allowed in a Team.

  • Time: Total 3 hr. will be given.

Rules & Regulations


  • You have to only use provided material. For any malpractices will result in disqualification.

  • A group shall not consist of more than 2 participants.

  • Use of mobile phones or any other electronic equipment during the competition is not permitted.

  • Early completion of the Planning will not add any advantage to the team but late submission will result in the deduction of points.

  • All participants are required to report 30 min. before the event starts, to the reporting desk.

  • Every participant should have his college identity card or at least one proof of identity. All the teams are required to bring the registration receipt with them.

  • In case of inconvenience, participants are required to seek any of the volunteers present.

  • Any misbehavior of a participant during the event may lead to elimination from the event he/she is currently in.

  • All the team members must be present during the event at the specified time. If the team is absent, it will be disqualified.

  • No extra time will be given to any team under any circumstances. The decision of the judges is final.

  • Organizers are not responsible for any loss in property, injury, and delays caused by participants during the event.

  • Other instructions will be provided on the day of the event.

  • Participants are required to stay up to date with the website.

  • All the rights are reserved with IGNITE-2023 committee.

Rounds With Names


  • There will be two rounds:
    • Round 1 will be of profile cutting, which is to be completed in 30-35 mins.

    • Round 2 will be of advanced profile cutting, which is to be completed in 20-25 mins.
